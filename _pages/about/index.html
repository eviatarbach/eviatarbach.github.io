I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in Mathematics of Environmental Data Science at the University of Reading, in the departments of Meteorology and Mathematics and Statistics. I am a part of the Data Assimilation Research Centre, the Centre for Mathematics of Planet Earth, and the National Centre for Earth Observation.

My research interests include data assimilation, predictability, machine learning, climate dynamics, nonlinear dynamics, and inverse problems. I am also interested more broadly in climate justice.
